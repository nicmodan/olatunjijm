SERVICES

Olatunji J Makinde & CO LTD is a modern, quality driven engineering firm providing a full range of construction services from conception to architectural designs, project management and commissioning of completed projects, we service various sectors of the Nigerian economy. We have the required depth of knowledge and technical skill within our team to deliver an efficient, effective and high-quality service at economic and competitive prices.
