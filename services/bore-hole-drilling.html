BORE HOLE DRILLING

We undertake the drilling and construction of bore hole for clean water.

The drilling process follows an initial soil test to ascertain if a location is viable for clean drinkable water, if the site contains clean water then we setup our drilling equipment and drill into the earth. Water sampling and assessment is carried out to determine the quality of the water based on its intended use. If all things are okay, then the pump and over-heading tank is installed.

Our drilling prices are competitive.
